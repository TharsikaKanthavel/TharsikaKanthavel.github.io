About Me

Data Science undergraduate at SLIIT Northern Uni, heading into my third year. I build things that actually work, not just models that perform well in a notebook.

My focus is machine learning, NLP, and AI research, working mainly in Python with Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn. I've been exploring practical applications of LLMs, most recently building an automatic recipe generation system that won Second Place at ThinkAI Expo 2025.

What sets my approach apart is that I don't stop at the model. With full-stack experience in the MERN stack, Spring Boot, and RESTful APIs, I can take a project from raw data — through preprocessing, EDA, and statistical modeling — all the way to a working, usable product.

Currently looking for a Data Science internship where I can apply these skills to real-world problems. Always happy to talk LLMs, messy datasets, or anything AI.
